Smart Closet Organiser

An AI-powered Android app that manages your wardrobe, plans your outfits, and suggests what to wear, based on your style and live weather conditions.

About Me & This Project

My name is Amy Comyns Haugh and I'm a final year BSc Software Systems Development Student. For my final year project I built a Smart Closet Organiser, which is an Android app that uses AI to help people manage their wardrobes and plan outfits.

The idea came from a pretty relatable problem. Research shows the average person only wears about 20% of their wardrobe 80% of the time. We all have clothes we forget about, can't find, or just never wear. I wanted to build something that actually fixes that, not just another organisation app, but one that uses AI and weather data to suggest what to wear and nudge you towards using what you already own.

It's built natively for Android using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, with Firebase for the backend and OpenWeatherMap for live weather data.

Key Features

The app has six main features, each one solving a real problem with wardrobe management

  • AI Outfit Recommendations

    The app analyses your wardrobe, your style preferences, and the live weather to suggest a full outfit for the day

  • Wardrobe Management

    Add and categorise your clothing by type, colour, size and season. Everything is searchable so you can actually find things quickly

  • Outfit Creation & Preview

    Mix and match items to build outfit combinations, save the ones you like, and get a visual preview before you wear them.

  • Calendar Integration

    Assign outfits to specific days so you can plan ahead for events, work, or just the week in general.

  • Smart Donation Tracker

    Tracks how often each item gets worn using metadata like "last worn" dates. If something hasn't been used in a while, the app suggests donating it, supporting sustainable fashion habits.

  • Weather-Aware Suggestions

    Pulls in real-time weather data via the OpenWeatherMap API so outfit suggestions are always practical for the day ahead.
